# Driftline in-game help ticker
# Pages are separated by a line with: ---

Controls: W/S thrust, A/D rotate.
Weapons: Space = primary fire. (Secondary fire is bound in Input Map.)
Abilities: Hold Shift + Z Stealth, X Cloak, C XRadar, V Antiwarp.
Help: F1 cycles pages. Hold Esc + press F6 toggles the ticker.

---

Energy: HP and resource share the same bar.
Damage reduces energy; weapons/abilities also consume energy.
Energy recharges after a short delay. Watch ENG and the recharge timer (R:).

---

Safe Zone: immune to damage while inside.
Safe Zone time can be limited; HUD shows SZ countdown when enabled.
Map/Radar: learn the arena lanes, doors, and safe-zone geometry.
